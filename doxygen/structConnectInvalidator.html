Detailed Description

A sample reduction pattern that replaces the right-hand-side of firrtl.connect and firrtl.strictconnect operations with a firrtl.invalidvalue.

This removes uses from the fanin cone to these connects and creates opportunities for reduction in DCE/CSE.
